Site Purpose

Site Name and Domain

The Temple Inn & Suites

templeinnsuites.com

Site Purpose

To provide a place that caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons.

Company Logo

Target Audience

Personas

Occupation: Temple Workers

Motivation to use this site: A temple worker is seeking information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers, while they serve in a nearby temple.

Occupation: Temple Patrons

Motivation to use this site: A temple patron is seeking information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers, while they participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips at a nearby temple.

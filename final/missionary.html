Suites

From standard suites to presidential/casino suites, there are a plethora of different types of hotel suites for the modern traveler to choose from at The Temple Inn & Suites.

Standard

This type of suite features a bedroom, bathroom, and a separate living space. The room comes in three different sizes: King, Queen, and Double.

Junior

This type of suite is a smaller version of the standard suites with no barriers between bed and living space.

Presidential

This type of suite includes separate sleeping and living quarters, state-of-the-art technology, high-tech security systems, a wet bar and prep kitchen, fireplaces, outdoor terraces and an oversized executive work desk.

Only two total in the whole hotel, reserve your presidential suite now!

Penthouse

Located on the top floor of our Temple Inn & Suites, our penthouse suites include a terrace, fireplace, more floor area than the junior and standard suites, oversized windows, den/office space, hot-tubs, and more.
